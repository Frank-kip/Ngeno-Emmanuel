Humble Appeal

My esteemed colleagues, I have a clear agenda for transforming our branch. I humbly request your prayers and support to set a track record that will earn your trust and potentially lead to re-election in the future.

Vision for KUPPET — Bomet Branch

As a seasoned unionist with a passion for teacher welfare and advocacy, I have a practical and pragmatic vision for the KUPPET-Bomet branch. My rich experience and unwavering commitment to teachers' interests will drive transformation in the areas listed below.

I. Interpretation & Enforcement of the Law

  • Democracy in BGC Representative Selection — strong communication channels, accountability, and democratic decision-making.
  • Enforcement of NGCW Guidelines — prompt response to welfare concerns, equity, transparency, and proper record-keeping.
  • Formation of Sectorial Committees — accountability, transparency, and responsible service delivery.
  • Clarifying Roles & Responsibilities — harmony, unity, and accountability in service delivery.

II. Management of Meetings

  • BEC meetings: once a month
  • BGC cum CMC meetings: once per school calendar term
  • BGA / AGM meetings: annually or special call

Regular meetings ensure smooth service delivery, reviews, and prompt responses to welfare and professional concerns.

III. Approvals of Financial Expenses

Transparent financial management with accountability, verifiability of funds, and the chair as a key signatory to operation and BBF accounts.

IV. Advocacy

  • Availability & Accessibility in meetings and advocacy for teachers’ welfare and professional growth.
  • Fostering Unity and building confidence in unionism.
